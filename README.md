## 📂 Projects & Assignments

### ✅ Excel — Data Cleaning, Analysis & Visualisation

#### 🛒 Jumia Product Performance Dashboard
> **Folder:** `Excel/Jumia_Product_Analysis/`

An end-to-end Excel data analysis project using a real-world Jumia e-commerce dataset containing product pricing, discounts, customer ratings, and reviews.

**What was done:**
- Data cleaning — removed exact duplicates, standardised formats, handled blank and ranged price values, resolved invalid entries
- Data enrichment — calculated absolute discount amounts, created Rating Category and Discount Category classification columns
- Descriptive statistics — averages across price, discount, and rating; most and least expensive products identified
- Trend analysis — explored relationships between discount percentage and reviews, and between product rating and reviews
- Product performance — Top 10 by discount, Top 10 by reviews, High vs Medium vs Low discount performance comparison
- Interactive dashboard — Pivot Tables, bar/line/pie charts, slicers, and conditional formatting

**Key insight:** Medium discount products (20–40%) attracted the highest average reviews, outperforming both heavily discounted and lightly discounted products — suggesting that aggressive discounting alone does not drive customer engagement.

**Tools used:** Microsoft Excel
